     <h1>Passive voice in present tense</h1>

     <p>Use the passive voice to emphasize the patient (the thing being acted upon) rather than the agent (the thing acting) in a sentence, by making it the sentence's subject. When the action is taking place right now, use the present tense passive voice. A sentence in present passive is constructed like this in German:</p><p><i>[patient]</i> wird <i>[preposition] [agent] [past participle of verb]</i>.</p><p>The active sentence "<i>Ich schreibe es</i>" ("<i>I am writing it</i>"), for example, becomes "<i>Es wird von mir geschrieben</i>" ("<i>It is being written by me</i>") in the present passive.</p><p>In this exercise, please transform the active sentences into passive ones. Click the "Send" button when finished. Our system will automatically correct errors and show the answers.</p>
